Tacos Recipe

Description

These classic ground beef tacos feature seasoned, juicy meat in warm, crispy shells, loaded with fresh tomatoes, shredded lettuce, and melted cheese. Prepared in just 20 minutes, they are a perfect, crowd-pleasing dinner for quick weeknights or weekend gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the packet instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
  4. Fill each taco shell with seasoned beef, then top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  5. Serve with sour cream and salsa if desired.
