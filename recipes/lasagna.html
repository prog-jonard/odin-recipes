Lasagna Recipe

Description

This ultimate homemade lasagna features tender pasta sheets layered with a rich, slow-simmered meat sauce and a creamy, three-cheese blend. Baked until bubbly with a golden-brown, cheesy crust, this comforting casserole is perfect for family dinners or entertaining.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  7. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
